Renovation

We renovate every room in the house

From bathroom to kitchen to bedroom, we can renovate any room to fit your dream. We have experience renovating over 100 projects and look forward to bring your idea to life.

If you are looking to create new rooms, new flooring, titling, or a complete makeover, then we are the right people for the job. We can create a beautiful, and high quality result that you will be able to enjoy for years to come.

As well, we take care to respect your house and time during a renovation. That's why we work efficiently to get the job done, while still maintaining that high quality result that we promise.

We look forward to discussing your dream renovation with you.

What we Renovate

Bathrooms, kitchens, living rooms, dining rooms, bedrooms, basements, and just about every other room in the house.

CARPENTRY

Services

Handcrafted for you.

Beyond renovation, we also offer carpentry services. We create high quality and custom wood workings, such as tables, chairs, and cabinetry.

With our extensive experience comes professional and high quality work. We hand craft every item, ensuring that it fits with your style and vision.

Some other examples of products that we have handcrafted are bookshelves, built-ins, side tables, and moldings.

Highest quality work

To ensure high quality work, we use top-of-the-grade materials and create a product with care and attention to detail. Naftali has created hundreds of cabinets, tables, chairs, bookshelves, and many other items. So he knows his craft. We are looking forward to discussing what we can create for you.

Custom design

Our work goes beyond manufactured items. We provide custom-made designs that fit with the style of your house. Additionally, we create a higher quality result with our work compared to manufactured items. This ensures a durability that will last daily wear and tear, and last a lifetime.
